How to imlement PWA

Progressive Web Applications (PWAs) are designed to provide users with a fast, reliable, and engaging experience, similar to native mobile applications. They use modern web technologies such as Service Workers and Web App Manifest to offer offline functionality, making them accessible and reliable in situations where users may have limited or unreliable internet connectivity.

PWAs are responsive, installable, and leverage native device features to provide an experience that is comparable to that of native apps, making them a popular choice for businesses looking to engage with their customers on the web.
